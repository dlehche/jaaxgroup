Company Introduction

Headquartered in Dubai Maritime City, JAAX Marine Engineering FZC delivers integrated maritime and offshore solutions through three focused divisions: genuine spare-parts supply, round-the-clock ship repair, and specialist oilfield services spanning drilling support and data processing. Our on-site workshops, Class-certified inventory, and multidisciplinary engineers guarantee minimum downtime, strict HSSE compliance, and transparent project controls.

Geoscience & Drilling Support Services

  • Seismic data processing: 2-D/3-D/4-D pre-stack depth migration, noise attenuation, AVO/AVA inversion, fracture detection.
  • VSP & LWD seismic: corridor stack, anisotropic tomography, time-depth tie for well positioning.
  • Wireline & LWD log interpretation: triple-combo, NMR, image logs, saturation-height modelling, TOC/brittleness for unconventionally.
  • Static & dynamic modelling: Petrel/Eclipse/INTERSECT geological models, history matching, uncertainty analysis, field-development scenarios.
  • While-drilling services: MWD real-time monitoring, geosteering, torque-and-drag modelling, pore-pressure prediction, casing-point selection, wiper-trip optimization and daily geo-reporting.
